Referral Program

(NOW LIVE)
The TradeGPT Referral Program is an exciting opportunity for our users to earn rewards by referring their friends and family to our platform. We believe that word of mouth is the best way to spread awareness about our product, and we want to reward our loyal users for helping us grow.
Our referral program offers a great opportunity for both the referrer and referee to earn rewards. When a new user signs up using a referral link, they will receive a 5% increase on their initial deposit, and the referring user will also receive a 5% based on their deposit. It's a win-win situation for both parties, and we encourage all users to take advantage of this program to maximize their earnings.
Our referral program is designed to be fair and transparent, with clear guidelines and no hidden fees. You can track your referrals and rewards in real-time through your account dashboard.
In addition to earning rewards, our referral program is also a great way to help your friends and family get started with crypto trading. We offer a user-friendly platform with advanced trading features and top-notch security, so you can feel confident referring your loved ones to our platform.
But that's not all. We want to reward our most active referrers even more, which is why we have a top tier for those who invite the most new users. These top tier referrers will receive additional rewards in our native token, providing even more incentive to invite others to join our community.
We believe that our referral program is a win-win for everyone involved: you earn rewards for spreading the word about our platform, your referrals benefit from our cutting-edge technology and exceptional customer support, and we are able to grow our user base and continue to improve our product.
